Custom fasteners, built to spec

When a standard screw will not do, C S Fasteners manufactures custom screws to your drawing or physical sample. Non-standard heads, drives, thread forms, materials and finishes — engineered to your performance and dimensional requirements.

What we can customize

  • Materials: mild steel, stainless, brass and alloy steel
  • Heads: any non-standard head profile
  • Drives: Philips, Pozi, Torx, Square, Allen or proprietary
  • Threads: custom pitch, lead, length and point geometry
  • Finishes: Zinc, Dacromet, Geo-met, Ruspert, Nickel, Chrome and more

White-label & OEM manufacturing

Produce fasteners under your own brand with custom packaging and full confidentiality. Ideal for distributors, importers and brand owners who want a reliable Indian manufacturing partner without disclosing their source.

Export-ready

Flexible MOQs, full export documentation and FOB / CIF shipping worldwide. Start from our standard self tapping, machine, wood and chipboard screw ranges, or send a drawing for a fully bespoke part.
