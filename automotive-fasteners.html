What automotive assembly requires

Automotive fasteners must hold tolerance, resist vibration loosening, and survive corrosion under the hood and underbody — all at production volume and consistency.

Recommended products

  • Machine screws — M2–M16, ISO coarse and fine pitch, DIN/ISO compliant.
  • Self tapping screws — for trim, panels, brackets and electrical fittings.
  • Custom screws — made to your drawing for specific assemblies.

Embrittlement-safe finishes

High-strength automotive screws can fail by hydrogen embrittlement after electroplating. Geo-met and Dacromet zinc-flake coatings are applied without electroplating, avoiding the risk while delivering high salt-spray performance — see the finishes guide.

Consistent tolerance, DIN/ISO compliance, flexible MOQ and OEM white-label supply, export-ready worldwide.
