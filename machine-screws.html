What are machine screws?

Machine screws are uniformly threaded fasteners designed to mate with a pre-tapped hole or a nut. Manufactured to tight tolerances, they deliver consistent, repeatable clamping for precision mechanical assemblies.

Common applications

  • Machinery, tooling and equipment assembly
  • Electrical panels, switchgear and enclosures
  • Automotive and engineering sub-assemblies
  • Instrumentation and precision hardware

Heads, drives & thread standards

Available in Pan, Cheese, Countersunk (CSK), Hex, Hex-Flange and Allen socket heads with Philips, Pozi, Torx, Allen and slotted drives. We thread to both ISO coarse and fine pitch with DIN/ISO compliance so parts integrate cleanly into your bill of materials.

Finishes

Zinc, Nickel, Chrome and Black Oxide / Phosphate finishes for appearance and corrosion resistance.
