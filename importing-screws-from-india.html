Importing Screws from India

Sourcing fasteners from India can cut costs significantly. Here is what international buyers should know about MOQ, lead times, shipping terms and documentation.

Request a Quote → WhatsApp Us

Why source screws from India

India combines competitive manufacturing costs with established export infrastructure and English-language business. For fasteners, that means good pricing on both standard and custom screws without sacrificing quality.

Minimum order quantity (MOQ)

MOQ depends on diameter, length and finish. As an MSME manufacturer, C S Fasteners offers flexible MOQs — you can start with a trial order and scale as demand grows, rather than committing to a full container on day one.

Lead times

Standard items ship faster than custom or white-label runs that require tooling and sampling. Always confirm a lead time in writing with your purchase order; we quote realistic timelines with every quotation.

Incoterms: FOB vs CIF

  • FOB (Free On Board): we deliver to the port and load the vessel; you arrange and pay for sea freight and insurance. Best if you have your own freight forwarder.
  • CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight): we cover freight and insurance to your destination port. Simpler if you are new to importing.

Export documentation

A typical fastener shipment from India includes:

  • Commercial invoice and packing list
  • Bill of lading
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Material / inspection certificate where required

Quality & packaging

Confirm material grade, finish and salt-spray requirement up front — see our finishes guide. Export packaging (cartons, pallets, moisture protection) is configured for long-distance sea freight, and white-label packaging is available.

How to get started

Send your specification — diameter, length, head and drive, finish and quantity — and we respond within 24 hours with pricing and lead time. Browse our product range to begin.
