What are self tapping screws?

Self tapping screws cut or form their own mating thread as they are driven, removing the need for a pre-tapped hole and dramatically speeding up assembly. They are the workhorse fastener for sheet metal, plastics and thin sections across countless industries.

Common applications

  • Sheet-metal enclosures, ducting and HVAC assemblies
  • Automotive trim, panels and electrical fittings
  • Appliances, electronics and white goods
  • Plastic housings and consumer products
  • General light fabrication and production lines

Head styles, drives & points

We manufacture Pan, Countersunk (CSK), Truss, Hex, Hex-Flange and Bugle heads, with additional profiles available on request. Drive recesses include Philips, Pozi, Torx (star), Square and slotted so you can match your existing tooling and torque needs. Point types span sharp, Type AB, Type A and self-drilling to suit different substrates and gauges.

Finishes for every environment

Standard Zinc Plated and Yellow Zinc for general use, plus high-performance Dacromet, Geo-met, Ruspert and Nickel coatings for corrosion-critical and export applications.
