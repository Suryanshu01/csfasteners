White-Label & OEM Manufacturing

Sell screws under your own brand without owning a factory. Here is how white-label and OEM fastener manufacturing works with C S Fasteners.

Request a Quote → WhatsApp Us

What is white-label manufacturing?

White-label manufacturing means we produce screws to your specification and package them under your brand name — your logo, your cartons, your part numbers. Your customers never see ours. OEM (original equipment manufacturer) supply is similar: we make the fastener that becomes part of your product or catalogue.

Who it is for

  • Distributors & importers building a private-label fastener line
  • Hardware brands that want a reliable manufacturing partner without capital investment
  • OEMs needing a specific screw for their assembly or kit

How the process works

  • 1. Specification — send a drawing, sample or our standard spec sheet.
  • 2. Sampling — we produce samples for approval before bulk.
  • 3. Production — manufacturing with in-house quality control.
  • 4. Branded packaging — your labels, cartons and documentation.
  • 5. Export — shipped worldwide on FOB or CIF terms.

Confidentiality & MOQ

Full confidentiality is standard — your brand and customers stay private. We offer flexible minimum order quantities so you can start small and scale, with custom packaging configured for your market.

Start your white-label line

Any product can be white-labelled: self tapping, machine, wood, chipboard and fully custom screws. Read our guide to importing screws from India for lead times and documentation.
