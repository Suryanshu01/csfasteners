What are chipboard screws?

Chipboard screws (also called particle-board or construction screws) use a deep, coarse, full-length thread and a thin shank to grip engineered wood without splitting it. The aggressive thread and sharp point make them fast to drive and extremely secure.

Common applications

  • Particle board, MDF and plywood assembly
  • Flat-pack and modular furniture
  • Cabinetry, shelving and interior fit-out
  • Decking and general construction

Heads, drives & points

Countersunk (CSK), Oval and Pan heads with Torx, Philips and Pozi drives. Torx is recommended for high-torque powered driving. Sharp or Type 17 points reduce splitting and remove the need for pilot holes.

Finishes

Zinc, Yellow Zinc and Black Oxide / Phosphate.
